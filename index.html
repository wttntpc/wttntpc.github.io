連結認知神經科學、運動科學與運動傷害防護，致力於探討身體活動與阻力健身運動對大腦執行功能的調控機制，進而改善高齡者與運動員的身心大腦健康。 Bridging the gap between Cognitive Neuroscience, Kinesiology, and Athletic Training. I investigate how physical activity and resistance exercise shape executive functions to optimize brain health and human performance.

研究與專長領域 Research & Specializations

認知神經科學 (Cognitive Neuroscience) Cognitive Neuroscience

探討大腦如何控制認知行為，特別是執行功能（如抑制控制、更新與計畫能力），並透過神經造影與心理測量解析運動對大腦可塑性的正向調節作用。

Investigating the neural substrates of executive functions (inhibitory control, updating, and planning). I examine how exercise induces brain plasticity using psychometric and neuroimaging perspectives.

健身運動與運動生理學 (Kinesiology & Exercise Science) Kinesiology & Exercise Science

研究不同強度、型態的健身運動（特別是急性阻力運動與血流限制訓練 BFR）對高階認知功能的劑量反應與生理調控模式。

Exploring physical activity dosage effects. I focus on resistance exercise, high-intensity interval training, and blood flow restriction (BFR) training on executive control.

運動傷害防護與高齡體適能 (Sports Medicine & Senior Fitness) Sports Injury Prevention & Geriatric Fitness

具備國家級「運動防護員」證照，整合肌力體能訓練與防護處方，提供運動員與銀髮族量身打造的健康體適能指引與運動防護實務。

As a certified Athletic Trainer, I design exercise prescriptions for older adults and athletes. I integrate injury prevention, active rehabilitation, and fitness training to promote health.
